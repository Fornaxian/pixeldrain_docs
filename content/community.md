Pixeldrain has a sorta kinda vibrant-ish community you can be a part of. Okay,
it's not very active, but we have our fun. Currently we mostly reside on
Discord: [discord invite link](https://discord.gg/TWKGvYAFvX).

The Discord community has an announcements channel which I use to announce large
changes to the site. There's also a support forum which we use to answer
people's questions (if the answer is not too obvious).

I (Fornax) am not a fan of centralized platforms overall. Discord has recently
started censoring certain topics. So, as an alternative I have also created a
Matrix community. It's currently not very active, but I will do my best to keep
an eye on it and engage in any conversations going on. Here's the [Matrix invite
link](https://matrix.to/#/#pixeldrain.com:matrix.org).
